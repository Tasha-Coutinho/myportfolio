Marirose Vegetable delivery App



service :

Web Development & Design

I designed a user-friendly vegetable delivery mobile app in Figma, featuring a clean, modern interface. The app allows users to browse fresh produce, view detailed product information, and easily add items to their cart. Key sections include a welcoming landing page, an organized store layout with categories for fresh vegetables and fruits, and product detail pages highlighting nutritional facts and pricing. I also included additional pages such as order history, payment methods, and a checkout flow to enhance the user experience. The design emphasizes simplicity, accessibility, and a healthy lifestyle, making it ideal for users looking for fresh produce delivered daily
